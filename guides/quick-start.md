# How to Create a Custom Website with WebStudio

Follow these steps to design and launch your custom website using WebStudio. This guide is tailored for small business owners and freelancers seeking a flexible web design experience.

## Step 1: Sign Up for WebStudio

1. Visit the [WebStudio website](https://www.webstudio.com).
2. Click on the “Get Started” button.
3. Fill in your email address and create a password.
4. Confirm your email through the verification link sent to your inbox.

## Step 2: Choose a Template

1. Log in to your WebStudio account.
2. Navigate to the “Templates” section from the dashboard.
3. Browse through the available templates that suit your business needs.
4. Click “Preview” to see how each template looks.
5. Select a template by clicking the “Use This Template” button.

## Step 3: Customize Your Design

1. Access the “Design” tab in the left sidebar.
2. Use the drag-and-drop editor to rearrange elements on your page.
3. Click on any element to edit its properties, such as text, images, and colors.
4. Upload your logo by clicking on the logo placeholder and selecting your image file.
5. Adjust the layout settings to fit your brand style.

## Step 4: Add Content

1. Navigate to the “Pages” section to manage your site’s pages.
2. Click “Add New Page” to create additional pages like About, Services, or Contact.
3. Use the text editor to input your content for each page.
4. Incorporate images and videos by dragging them into the content area.
5. Ensure each page has a clear call-to-action button.

## Step 5: Optimize for SEO

1. Go to the “Settings” section in the left sidebar.
2. Select “SEO” from the dropdown menu.
3. Enter your website title, meta description, and keywords relevant to your business.
4. Enable the “Friendly URLs” option for better search engine visibility.
5. Save your changes.

## Step 6: Preview and Publish

1. Click on the “Preview” button to see how your website appears on different devices.
2. Make any final adjustments based on the preview.
3. Once satisfied, click the “Publish” button to make your website live.
4. Share your website link on social media and with your network.

## Step 7: Manage Your Website

1. Return to the WebStudio dashboard to manage your website.
2. Use the “Analytics” section to track visitor statistics.
3. Regularly update content and images to keep your site fresh.
4. Utilize the “Support” section for any technical assistance you may need.

By following these steps, you will create a customized website that stands out from competitors like Wix, Squarespace, and Weebly. Leverage WebStudio’s flexibility to enhance your online presence effectively.
