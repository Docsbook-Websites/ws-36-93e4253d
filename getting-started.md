## What You'll Learn
In this tutorial, you will learn how to set up your first website using WebStudio. You will explore the intuitive design tools and advanced customization options that make WebStudio a versatile platform for both beginners and experienced developers.

## Prerequisites
- **WebStudio Account**: Sign up for a free account at [WebStudio](https://www.webstudio.com).
- **Browser**: Use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Safari for optimal performance.
- **Basic HTML/CSS Knowledge**: Familiarity with HTML and CSS will enhance your customization experience.

## Steps to Get Started

1. **Log into Your WebStudio Account**
   - Go to the WebStudio website and click on the "Login" button. Enter your credentials to access your dashboard.

2. **Create a New Project**
   - From your dashboard, click on the "Create New Project" button. Choose a template that fits your design vision. WebStudio offers a variety of templates tailored for different industries.

3. **Customize Your Template**
   - Use the drag-and-drop editor to modify your template. Click on elements to change text, images, and colors. Explore the sidebar for layout options and design tools.

4. **Add New Pages**
   - Click on the "Pages" tab in the sidebar. Select "Add Page" to create additional pages for your website. Name each page and choose a layout.

5. **Integrate Functionalities**
   - Use WebStudio’s built-in features to add functionalities like contact forms, galleries, and e-commerce options. Navigate to the "Add Features" section and select the desired functionalities.

6. **Preview Your Website**
   - Click on the "Preview" button to see how your website looks on different devices. Make adjustments as necessary to ensure a responsive design.

7. **Publish Your Website**
   - Once satisfied with your design, click the "Publish" button. Choose a domain name or connect an existing one. Confirm your settings and publish your site to go live.

8. **Monitor Performance**
   - After publishing, use the analytics tools provided by WebStudio to track visitor engagement and performance metrics. Adjust your content based on the insights gained.
