## What You'll Learn
In this tutorial, you will learn how to create your first website using WebStudio. You will explore the platform's design tools, customize your site, and publish it online.

## Prerequisites
- A WebStudio account (sign up at [WebStudio](https://www.webstudio.com))
- A modern web browser (Google Chrome version 90 or later, Mozilla Firefox version 88 or later)
- Basic understanding of web design concepts

## Steps to Create Your First Website

1. **Log In to Your WebStudio Account**
   - Open your web browser and navigate to [WebStudio](https://www.webstudio.com).
   - Click on the "Log In" button and enter your credentials.

2. **Choose a Template**
   - After logging in, click on the "Create New Site" button.
   - Browse through the available templates. Select one that fits your vision by clicking on it.

3. **Customize Your Template**
   - Use the drag-and-drop editor to modify your chosen template. 
   - Click on elements like text boxes, images, and buttons to edit their content and styles.
   - Access the "Design" panel on the left to change colors, fonts, and layouts.

4. **Add Pages to Your Site**
   - Click on the "Pages" tab in the top menu.
   - Select "Add New Page" and choose the type of page you want (e.g., About, Services).
   - Customize each page using the same drag-and-drop tools.

5. **Set Up Navigation**
   - Go to the "Navigation" section in the menu.
   - Drag your pages into the navigation bar to create a user-friendly menu structure.

6. **Preview Your Site**
   - Click on the "Preview" button to see how your website looks on different devices.
   - Make any necessary adjustments based on the preview.

7. **Publish Your Website**
   - Once satisfied with your site, click on the "Publish" button.
   - Choose a domain name or connect an existing one.
   - Confirm your settings and click "Publish" again to make your site live.

8. **Share Your Website**
   - After publishing, share your new website link on social media or with clients to showcase your work.
